Orange's Favorite Games

No. 1 Elden Ring

Elden Ring

FromSoftware's Elden Ring offers a sprawling open world filled with rich lore, brutal combat, and deep RPG mechanics. Set in the hauntingly beautiful Lands Between, players explore vast landscapes, uncover ancient secrets, and battle formidable foes in a journey shaped by their choices. With a seamless open world, intricate dungeons, and a sense of freedom rarely seen in the genre, Elden Ring delivers an unforgettable adventure where every challenge feels rewarding, and every victory is hard-earned.

This game is my first introduction to the souls genre. It was simply a mindblowing experience.

No. 2 Ghost of Tsushima

Ghost of Tsushima

Ghost of Tsushima transports players to 13th-century Japan, where samurai Jin Sakai must adapt his honor-bound ways to resist the Mongol invasion. Blending fast-paced katana combat with stealth mechanics, the game allows players to fight as a noble samurai or embrace the path of the Ghost. With a breathtaking open world inspired by classic samurai films, a deeply emotional story, and a stunningly immersive atmosphere, Ghost of Tsushima is a poetic and powerful tribute to Japanese history and culture.

This is the prettiest game I've played in terms of graphics and it has such a satisfying combat system.

No. 3 The Last of Us Part 1

The Last of Us Part 1

A harrowing yet beautiful journey through a post-apocalyptic America, The Last of Us Part 1 tells the story of Joel and Ellie as they struggle to survive in a world ravaged by a deadly fungal outbreak. With gripping emotional storytelling, lifelike characters, and tense, strategic combat, the game delivers an experience that is as heart-wrenching as it is thrilling. Every moment is filled with raw human emotion, making it one of the most compelling and unforgettable narratives in gaming history.

This game had me sobbing by the end.

No. 4 Zelda BOTW

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Breath of the Wild revolutionized open-world gaming with its vast, living world and boundless sense of adventure. As Link, players can explore a ruined yet breathtaking Hyrule, climb any mountain, glide across valleys, and experiment with a physics-driven environment. With rewarding exploration, challenging puzzles, and emergent gameplay that encourages creativity, Breath of the Wild offers an unparalleled sense of freedom that makes every journey through its world unique.

This game is just so wholesome.

No. 5 Hollow Knight

Hollow Knight

A mesmerizing blend of atmospheric storytelling and challenging gameplay, Hollow Knight takes players deep into the ruins of Hallownest, a once-great kingdom now overrun by forgotten creatures and deadly secrets. With fluid combat, precise platforming, and a vast interconnected world filled with haunting beauty, the game invites players to uncover its mysteries at their own pace. A true Metroidvania masterpiece, Hollow Knight rewards exploration, patience, and skill, creating an unforgettable experience that lingers long after the journey ends.

This is my favorite indie game.
